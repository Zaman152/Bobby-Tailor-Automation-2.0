<threat_model>
ASVS L1:
- **Cost runaway (MEDIUM):** retries multiply on noisy sheets. Mitigations: ≤2 retries/item hard cap, every retry charged to the run BudgetGuard, retry pass skipped entirely once budget exhausted (items stay flagged).
- **Silent flag suppression (design HIGH, mitigated):** a retry may relabel semantics or confirm; it must never delete a flag without a recorded resolution — retry_history is mandatory on any changed item (acceptance-tested).
- ID/choice-grounded schema prevents free-text answer injection.
</threat_model>
